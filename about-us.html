About Us

Learn more about us

Altus IT Solutions is Windsor's newest IT Support & Service company. Created in April of 2021, we saw that there was no real solution for affordable IT solutions for the average home and small business. We decided to fill that gap. Our mission is to bring affordable IT services to the average consumer while keeping the project as simple as possible for you to understand. We are striving to be your first choice in an IT company by bringing consumer transparency back to the IT Service industry.
