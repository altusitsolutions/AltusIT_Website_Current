Website Design & Development

Whether you're a small business, a hobbyist, or just someone who wants a little piece of the internet to themselves, a website is a big part of your online identity. A website is your first impression to a lot of people.

We can make that impression last.

We make sure that your web identity is custom to your needs while still keeping up with the trends and standards of modern website design and development. We work with you every step of the way to make your vision come to life.

Below is a list of the services we can offer you when it comes to making your identity known.

  • Custom coded website — This option is best for a website that doesn't really change much
  • Wordpress hosted/Wordpress custom theme — We build your site with a completely custom theme or by using a current theme. This is a great option if your site is going to be featuring new content a lot
  • Wordpress drag and drop website — This option is best for those who want to be able to update their site without any coding knowledge
  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization) — using Google Analytics, we make data-driven changes to your site to maximize the amount of visitors and improve your place on search engines.
  • Website updates — Best choice if you need some changes made to your current site but aren't sure how to do them yourself
  • For additional services, please contact us.
